## Core concepts (high-level)

- **AI > ML > DL > GenAI** (nested structure)
- **Supervised learning**: with labels
- **Unsupervised learning**: without labels
- **Classification**: categorical target
- **Regression**: numerical target
- **Training vs inference**
- **Overfitting vs underfitting**

---

## Exam focus

You should be able to explain:

1. When do you choose classification vs regression?
2. Why do we need a train/validation/test split?
3. What is the difference between loss and metric?
4. How do you detect overfitting from results?
5. Why is there no single best algorithm for all problems?
